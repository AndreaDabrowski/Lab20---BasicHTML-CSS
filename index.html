Andrea's Animals:


Meet Schmee!

I adopted Schmee almost two years ago because he was the sweetest cat at the shelter. Shortly after bringing him home, I realized he was actually a huge jerk! The 'ol bait and switch! But I love him... He's really good at being obese and eating large amounts of food in one sitting. He's also loves cuddling all night long.


Meet Taz!

Taz is 14 years old, and extremely handsome for his age. I adopted him about 2 months ago because he was the sweetest cat I could find on Petfinder. Shortly after bringing him home, I realized that he actually is the nicest cat in the entire world. He has severe dementia, so he frequently meows at walls. I make fun of him for it, but he doesn't care because he is also deaf.
